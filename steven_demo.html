Grid Adventurer

About

Grid Adventurer is an arcade game made with pure Javascript followed by HTML and CSS. The goal of this game is for the player to explore a 25x25 grid area and find the red flag in each stage within a limited amount of energy and health. You also can collect loot such as money to get more points.

Each action consumes stamina such as moving, breaking obstacles, hunting enemies. Running out of stamina will use your health which depletes much faster. Hazards will harm the player reducing health directly. Enemies will attack and deplete health.

Arrows help guide where the red flag are, though higher stages will decrease the occurence of arrows

Getting the red flag takes you to the next stage where you repeat the same objective. Though it becomes slightly harder.

The player plays the game until they lose. The game is over when the player loses all of their health.

Have fun and hope you enjoy my first javascript program!

Items

Bomb - creates a 5x5 explosion centering around the bomb that wipes obstacles, hazards, and enemies (but not walls). Bombs must be placed at a tile that has already been explored

Firecracker - wipes obstacles, hazards, and enemies (but not walls) vertically or horizontally at the player's location.

Drone - reveals a 3x3 location at any location of your choice.

Lightning - randomly strikes 20 tiles on either an unseen area, enemy, or hazard. Enemies and Hazards are instantly destroyed.

Goggle - the next 50 moves allows you to navigate through walls and collect items behind obstacles and hazards.

Instructions

Keyboard

  • Arrow keys to move around. It can also be used to interact with objects by first marking it and then interacting with it
  • Q to use a bomb. Arrow keys to control where the bomb goes.
  • W to use a firecracker. Up/Down for vertical, Left/Right for horizontal.
  • E to use a drone. Arrow keys to control where the drone goes.
  • R to use lightning. Press Enter again to activate it
  • T to activate goggles. Press Enter again to activate it
  • P resets the game.
  • Escape to cancel the use of Items.
  • Enter to use the Items.

Mouse/Phone

  • Tap on the cell near the player to move the character. Interaction can be done by clicking on the object twice.
  • Click the Item text to use them. For bombs and drone, click at a cell on where to use it. For firecracker, click a cell where it is either vertically or horizontally towards the player to use it.
  • To cancel, click the respective text again.
