About Me

Hello there, let me introduce myself. I am Steven Wang and I have recently graduated from California University of Long Beach in Spring 2019 with a Bachelors with a Computer Engineering degree. While I was in college, I have done several hands-on projects in between both software and hardware and making things happen. I have developed personal skills from work and myself that universally carries over between now and future. I have dealt with configuring computers such as creating emails for users, setting up new computers for users, as well as troubleshooting computers that are not working properly. Outside of work, I have proficiency in C/C++, and JavaScript. I also have some several hands-on experience onto other programming languages out there such as Python, MATLAB, SQL and Verilog. I can use a version source control called Git to be able to see myself and other changes made to files.

On my free time, I like to draw, walking, listening to music, solving sudoku puzzles and various math problems. I also code my own software programs to expand my mindset from time to time.
